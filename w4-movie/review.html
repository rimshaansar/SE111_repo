Review

Rimsha Ansar March 19, 2022

This movie was really amazing, and very enjoable. It is a time-less movie, because we are able to relate to the character, it was perfectly displayed. The animations were good. I highly reccomend this movie it is full of plot-twists,comedy,action, and a character development that leaves you wanting more.

My favourite character was Gru, who was also the main character. He had a poor upbringing, as his mother never acknowledged his effort or showed affection towards him. In spite of that, he aims to become the greatest villian of all time to have her realize his worth for once. On his quest, he adopts 3 orphan girls for a crucial part of his plan. But soon learns to return the love they gave him and finds his true happines. He still stole the moon, though.

Trivia

  • Did you know that the movie first went under the name "Evil Me"?
  • Steve Carell described Gru's accent as a mixture of Ricardo Montalban and Bela Lugosi.
  • Directors Pierre Coffin amd Chris Renaud developed 'minionese' language heard in the movie. It's formed of multiple dialects including Spanish, French and Russian.
